Golf Clubs: The Heart of the Game

Golf clubs are specialized equipment used to strike a golf ball, and they come in various types, such as drivers, irons, and putters, each designed for different shots. The design and material of the club influence its performance, with modern clubs often incorporating advanced technology to enhance accuracy and distance.

Other Essential Equipment

Beyond clubs, golfers utilize a range of equipment to enhance their game:

  • Golf Balls: Varying in construction and performance characteristics.
  • Golf Shoes: Providing stability and traction.
  • Golf Bags: Carrying clubs and accessories.
  • Golf Gloves: Improving grip and preventing blisters.
